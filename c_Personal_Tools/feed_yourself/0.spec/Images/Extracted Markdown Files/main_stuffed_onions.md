# Stuffed Onions

> Layers upon layers of sweet, tangy, jewel-filled goodness. The pomegranate and silan combo is pure Middle Eastern magic. These onions have more personality than most people.

## Ingredients

### Onions
- 6-7 purple and white onions, cooked in their skins until fork-tender

### Filling
- 2 large onions, cut into tiny cubes
- 3 small carrots, grated
- 3-4 tbsp uncooked basmati rice
- 3 tbsp uncooked quinoa
- 1 tbsp uncooked brown or black lentils
- 2 tbsp cranberries or raisins
- 2 tbsp pine nuts
- 2 tbsp pomegranate concentrate
- 2 tbsp silan (date syrup)
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 1/2 tsp sumac
- 1 bunch mint and parsley, finely chopped
- Zest of 1 lemon

### Decoration
- Olive oil
- Cherry tomatoes
- Tiny sprigs of mint

### Sauce
- 1 liter water
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 tbsp silan
- Salt and pepper to taste
