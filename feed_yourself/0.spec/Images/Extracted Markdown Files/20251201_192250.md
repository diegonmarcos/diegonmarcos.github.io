Lentil and sweet potato salad
Ingredients:
Black lentils (you can also replace with the same amount of quinoa)
Sweet potato cut into cubes
olive oil salt pepper for seasoning
Parsley, coriander mint, rocket leaves. The most fun is if there are many!
For the sauce - a third of olive oil, a third of silane, a little more than a third of lemon juice, salt, pepper to taste
Method:
Cook the lentils. At the same time, bake the sweet potatoes in the oven. We will chop all the greens.
After all the ingredients have cooled, mix everything together and season.
Take a capsicum
Eat it whole
Avoid the inside part
