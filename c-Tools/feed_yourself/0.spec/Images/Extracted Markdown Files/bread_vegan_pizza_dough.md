# Vegan Pizza Dough

> Four perfect pizza bases from scratch, no animal products required. Crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside. Friday night sorted.

**Yield:** 4 pizza bases

## Ingredients

- 4 to 4 1/2 cups flour
- 3 tsp sugar
- 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
- 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk together yeast, sugar and water. Set aside for 5 minutes until foamy
2. In another bowl, sift together the flour and salt
3. Make a well in the center of the flour and add the yeast mixture and oil
4. Mix together with a wooden spoon
5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5 minutes, until smooth and not sticky
6. Add more flour (1 tbsp at a time) if dough is sticky, or add 1 tsp water at a time if too dry
7. Place dough in an oiled bowl and cover with a clean towel
8. Leave to rise for 45 minutes
9. Divide into 4 portions and roll out on a floured surface
10. Add sauce, cheese and toppings
11. Bake for 12 minutes at 220Â°C
