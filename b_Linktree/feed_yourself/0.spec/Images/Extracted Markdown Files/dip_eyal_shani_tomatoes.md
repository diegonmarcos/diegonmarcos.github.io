# Eyal Shani Tomatoes

> The famous Israeli chef's deceptively simple tomato dish that'll blow your mind. Crushed tomatoes, sour cream, olive oil, challah. That's it. That's the whole genius.

## Ingredients

- Tomatoes (ripe and beautiful)
- Olive oil
- Coarse salt
- Black pepper
- Sour cream
- Fresh za'atar or mint leaves
- A slice of challah or good bread

## Instructions

1. Crush tomatoes in a blender with salt and pepper
2. Pour the tomato pur√©e into a deep, fun plate
3. Put a dollop of sour cream in the middle
4. Drizzle generously with olive oil
5. Sprinkle with coarse salt and black pepper
6. Add fresh za'atar or mint leaves
7. Eat with a slice of challah
8. Question why you ever made anything more complicated
