# George's Birthday Carrot Cake

> The legendary carrot cake that's become a birthday tradition. Moist, spiced, loaded with pineapple and nuts, topped with tangy cream cheese frosting. Birthdays were made for this.

## Ingredients

### Cake
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup crushed pineapple (canned)
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups oil
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups chopped nuts
- 2 cups grated carrot

### Cream Cheese Frosting
- 100g cream cheese
- 3 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1 1/2 cups icing sugar
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- Juice of 1/4 lemon

## Instructions

### Make the Cake
1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C
2. Line a tin with baking paper
3. Mix all dry ingredients together
4. In a separate bowl, mix eggs, oil, and vanilla
5. Combine wet and dry ingredients
6. Beat all for 5 minutes
7. Fold in pineapple, nuts and carrots
8. Pour into prepared tin
9. Bake for 1 to 1.5 hours (until done - test with a skewer)

### Make the Frosting
1. Cream the butter and cream cheese together
2. Add icing sugar, vanilla and lemon juice
3. Beat until smooth
4. Spread generously over cooled cake
