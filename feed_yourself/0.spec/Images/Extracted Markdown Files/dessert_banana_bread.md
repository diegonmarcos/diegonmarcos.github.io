# Banana Bread

> The classic banana bread that rescues overripe bananas from the bin. Walnuts and chocolate chips are "optional" but let's be real - they're not optional. Don't overmix or the banana gods will be angry.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups mashed bananas (about 4 bananas)
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup melted butter or vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs (or 2 flax/chia eggs for vegan)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (strongly recommended!)
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips (strongly recommended!)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C
2. Grease a loaf pan
3. In a large bowl, whisk together mashed banana, sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla
4. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg
5. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
6. Stir until JUST combined - don't overmix!
7. Fold in walnuts and chocolate chips
8. Pour batter into prepared pan
9. Top with more walnuts and sliced banana if you're feeling fancy
10. Bake for 50-60 minutes
11. Test with a fork - it should come out clean and top should spring back
