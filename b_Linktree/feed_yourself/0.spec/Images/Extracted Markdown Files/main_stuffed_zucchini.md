# Stuffed Zucchini

> Little green tubes of herby, lemony, rice-filled happiness. The yogurt dip takes it from great to unforgettable. Perfect for impressing people you want to like you.

## Ingredients

### Zucchini
- 12-14 medium hard crunchy zucchinis (or 25 small ones)

### Filling
- Olive oil for frying
- 2 onions, diced
- 3/4 cup round rice
- 1 small carrot, grated
- 1 cup mixed herbs (parsley and mint), finely chopped
- Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp sumac

### Sauce
- 1 liter water
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 1 tbsp coarse salt
- Black pepper, finely ground

### Yogurt Dip
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (or sheep/goat yogurt)
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp salt
- Black pepper
- Sumac
- 1 ripe tomato, squeezed with juice
- 1/2 tsp lemon zest
- Dill and mint for decoration
