# Sweet Potato and Goat Cheese Pie Filling

> Creamy, sweet, tangy perfection. The pine nuts add crunch while goat cheese brings the funk. This filling turns any pie into a showstopper.

## Ingredients

- 50g butter
- 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1.5cm cubes
- 1 roll goat cheese, cut into same-sized cubes
- 250ml cooking cream
- 1/2 cup roasted pine nuts
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
- 3 eggs, beaten
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of black pepper

## Instructions

1. Fry sweet potato cubes in butter until softened and golden
2. Let cool
3. Mix all filling ingredients together
4. Add the cooled sweet potatoes
5. Pour over pre-baked pie dough
6. Bake at 180°C for 35-40 minutes until set

# Onion and Feta Cheese Pie Filling

> Caramelized onion heaven meets salty feta. The thyme adds an earthy note that'll make people think you went to culinary school. You didn't, but they don't need to know.

## Ingredients

- 8-9 large onions
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 2 stalks fresh thyme
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 packet sweet cream
- 150g feta cheese
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Pinch of nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Fry garlic and onions in olive oil on medium-low heat
2. Cook until onions turn a honey color (this takes time and patience!)
3. Place onion mixture on frozen pie dough (no pre-baking needed for this filling)
4. Mix eggs, cream, nutmeg, salt and pepper
5. Pour over the onions
6. Crumble feta on top
7. Bake at 200°C for about 40 minutes
