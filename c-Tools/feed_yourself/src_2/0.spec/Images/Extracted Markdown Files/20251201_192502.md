OTHER
Granola
Ingredients:
500 grams of thick oats
Four cups of coarsely chopped nut mixture (pecans, walnuts, hazelnuts, cashews) Pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds
Half a cup of oil
Half a cup of honey/silan if you want it to be vegan
a little salt
Method:
Mix all the ingredients, spread the mixture on one or two trays.
bake (180 degrees Celsius) for half an hour in total, when every ten minutes we take it out to mix and return it to the oven. Keep an eye on the stove, make sure it doesn't burn. If you want, you can add coconut chips or dried bananas in the last ten minutes.
