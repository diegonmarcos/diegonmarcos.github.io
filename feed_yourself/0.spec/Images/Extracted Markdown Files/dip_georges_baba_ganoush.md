# George's Baba Ganoush

> Smoky, creamy, lemony perfection. The key is roasting those eggplants until they're properly charred and draining ALL the liquid. Tahini makes everything better - this is scientific fact.

## Ingredients

- 2 medium eggplants
- 2 medium garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1/2 cup olive oil (plus more for brushing and garnish)
- 2 tbsp parsley
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- Pinch of smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 230Â°C
2. Halve eggplants and place on a parchment-lined baking tray
3. Brush cut sides with olive oil and place cut-side DOWN
4. Roast for 35-40 minutes
5. Set aside to cool for a few minutes
6. Scoop out the middle, discard the skin
7. Use a mesh strainer and mash eggplant until ALL liquid comes out
8. Transfer to a bowl, add garlic and lemon
9. Mix with a fork until eggplant breaks down
10. Add tahini and stir until combined
11. Slowly add olive oil while stirring
12. Mix until pale and creamy
13. Add salt, cumin and parsley
14. Taste and adjust salt/lemon as needed
15. Serve drizzled with olive oil, sprinkled with parsley and paprika
