# Noa's Cabbage Salad

> Crunchy, nutty, and dangerously addictive. The Asian-inspired dressing transforms humble cabbage into the star of the show. Warning: you'll eat the whole bowl.

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 1 whole cabbage, cut as thinly as you can be bothered to
- Pine nuts (or sub with more sunflower seeds)
- Sunflower seeds
- Pumpkin seeds
- Slivered almonds
- Sesame seeds
- Cashews (optional)

### Dressing
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 1/3 cup white vinegar
- 1-2 tbsp sugar (to taste)

## Instructions

1. Roast each type of seed INDIVIDUALLY in a frying pan (so different seeds don't burn), without olive oil unless specified
2. Put dressing ingredients in a good jar and shake well until sugar dissolves
3. Keep everything separate until right before eating
4. Toss together and enjoy! <3

# Beit Elisha Salad

> The "use what you got" salad philosophy at its finest. No rules, no measurements, just pure veggie chaos dressed to impress. Fridge cleanout never tasted so good.

## Ingredients

- Every vegetable that's available
- Olive oil
- Lemon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Sumac

## Instructions

1. Take every vegetable that's available
2. Cut to your preferable size
3. Dress with olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper and sumac
4. Enjoy!
