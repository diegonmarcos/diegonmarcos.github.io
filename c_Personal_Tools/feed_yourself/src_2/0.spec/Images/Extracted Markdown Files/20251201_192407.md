Aloo Gobi - Indian cauliflower and vegetables
Ingredients:
Method:
cauliflower broken into large florets
2 potatoes cut into cubes
Red pepper cut into strips
Diced pumpkin/sweet potato
A large onion cut into thin rings
hot pepper cut into rings (optional)
3 crushed garlic cloves
2 cm grated ginger
A ripe tomato cut into segments
Half a teaspoon of cumin seeds (optional)
Half a teaspoon of coriander seeds (optional)
A cup of finely chopped cilantro and basil (mandatory!)
Juice of half a lemon
A cup of boiling water mixed with:
。 Teaspoon of salt
O
A teaspoon of black pepper
。 A teaspoon of turmeric
O
A teaspoon of cumin
。 A teaspoon of hot paprika
O
In a flat and not hot pot with a little olive oil, fry the onion, hot pepper, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds and coriander
When the pot starts to sizzle, add vegetables: cauliflower florets, potatoes, pumpkin, ripe tomato
Add the cup of boiling water seasoned with turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt and black pepper.
Add a cup of herbs, mix
Bring to a boil, lower the heat, cover the pot, 50 minutes on a low heat.
Taste and adjust the seasoning, transfer to the oven, a pot without a lid, another 30
minutes at 200 degrees until golden, garnish with more herbs and sprinkle juice from half a lemon.
