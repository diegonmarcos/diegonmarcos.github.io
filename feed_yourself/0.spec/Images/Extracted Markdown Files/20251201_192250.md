# Lentil and Sweet Potato Salad

> Sweet meets earthy in this colorful powerhouse of a salad. The silan dressing adds a touch of Israeli magic. Meal prep champion right here.

## Ingredients

### Salad
- Black lentils (or substitute with quinoa)
- Sweet potato, cut into cubes
- Olive oil, salt, pepper for seasoning
- Parsley, coriander, mint, rocket leaves (the more herbs the merrier!)

### Dressing
- 1/3 olive oil
- 1/3 silan (date syrup)
- A little more than 1/3 lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook the lentils
2. At the same time, bake the sweet potatoes in the oven
3. Chop all the greens
4. After all ingredients have cooled, mix everything together
5. Add dressing and season to taste
