# Chard and Eggplant Fritters

> Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, packed with hidden veggies. These green-speckled beauties are basically a salad you can fry. Healthy-ish comfort food at its finest.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium eggplant, peeled and diced
- 1 bunch chard/mangold, roughly chopped (without hard white part)
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 zucchini, grated
- 1 potato, grated
- 1 onion, cut into tiny cubes
- Finely chopped herbs (parsley, mint, basil, oregano - your choice)
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs (optional - for vegan, add more flour or make a quick puree)
- 2 tbsp chickpea/wheat/lentil flour

### Spices
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp paprika

## Instructions

1. Cook chard and eggplant in boiling water until soft
2. Drain in a strainer for 30 minutes to remove excess liquid
3. Chop the cooked vegetables with a knife
4. Squeeze grated carrot and zucchini well to remove all liquid
5. In a large bowl, mix all vegetables together
6. Add spices, eggs, flour and breadcrumbs
7. Form into beautiful balls
8. Refrigerate for 30 minutes

### To Fry
- Deep fry in hot oil until golden

### To Bake
- Line a baking tray with parchment paper
- Brush with olive oil
- Place balls on tray
- Brush tops with more olive oil
- Bake at high heat until golden
