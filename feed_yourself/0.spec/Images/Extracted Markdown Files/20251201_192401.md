# Noa's Tofu Shawarma

> The vegan shawarma that fooled carnivores everywhere. Crispy, spicy, and legitimately delicious. Stuff it in a pita with hummus and you won't miss the meat. We promise.

*Note: This spice mix also works for 1kg chicken breast/thighs - marinate and grill!*

## Ingredients

- 1 block tofu (300g)
- 2 heaped tsp garlic powder
- 2 heaped tsp sweet paprika
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 3/4 tsp cumin (be careful - it's strong!)
- 1 tsp ground coriander seed (skip if you don't have)
- Cayenne pepper to taste
- Salt to taste
- Juice of half a lemon
- Olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix all the spices in a bowl
2. Add enough olive oil to wet all the spices
3. Squeeze in half a lemon
4. Squeeze out excess water from the tofu into the sink
5. Grate the tofu straight into your spice mix
6. Mix well until tofu is coated
7. Spread out thinly onto a lined baking tray
8. Bake at 180-190Â°C fan forced for 15-20 minutes
9. Keep an eye on it - you want it REALLY crispy!
10. Eat on its own or stuff in a pita with hummus and Israeli salad
11. Fool yourself into thinking it's real shawarma
12. Enjoy!
