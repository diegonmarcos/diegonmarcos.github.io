# Roasted Pepper Spread

> Sweet, smoky, and utterly addictive. The oregano is non-negotiable - don't you dare skip it. Perfect on everything from bread to humans (okay, maybe just bread).

## Ingredients

- 4-5 red peppers
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 flat tsp dry oregano (don't skip this!)
- 1 spoonful quality balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- Juice of half a lemon
- 1 flat tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 spoonful maple syrup/silan/honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C
2. Roast whole peppers for about 30 minutes until you see charred areas
3. Turn them after 15 minutes if needed
4. Let cool, then remove seeds and skin
5. Meanwhile, mix all other ingredients in a bowl
6. Put roasted peppers and dressing mixture in a blender
7. Blend until smooth
8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed
9. Serve with bread, crackers, or your finger (no judgment)
