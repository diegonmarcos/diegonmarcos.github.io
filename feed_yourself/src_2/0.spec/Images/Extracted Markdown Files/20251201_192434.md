Eyal shani
Ingredients:
Tomatoes
Method:
Olive oil
Coarse salt
black pepper
Sour cream
Fresh zaatar/mint leaves for decoration and taste
A slice of challah from Friday dinner or some bread (if you want to make your own - see the bread section in the recipe book)
Using a blender, crush the tomatoes, with some salt and pepper.
Pour the tomato paste into a deep and fun plate, put sour cream in the middle. Drizzle
over olive oil, sprinkle salt and pepper to taste and add fresh thyme leaves. Eat with a slice of challah.
