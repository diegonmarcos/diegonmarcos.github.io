DESSERTS & CAKES
George's birthday & biography carrot cake
Ingredients for the cake:
•
21⁄2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 1⁄2 tsp baking soda (bicarbonate of soda)
1 1⁄2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup crushed pineapple (canned)
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups chopped nuts
•
2 cups grated carrot
Preheat the oven to 160°C
Mix dry ingredients
Add eggs, oil, vanilla (mix first separately and then combine with dry ingredients)
Beat all for five minutes
Fold in the pineapple, nuts and carrots
Pour mixture into tin line with baking paper
Bake for 1-1.5 hours (until it's done)
Ingredients for the frosting/icing:
•
100g cream cheese
3 tbs butter (unsalted)
1 1⁄2 cups icing sugar
1⁄2 tsp vanilla
Juice 1/4 of a lemon
Cream the butter and the butter and cream cheese
Add icing sugar, vanilla and lemon
