Ingredients:
Rina's Matzah Brittle
4-5 sheets of matzah (or however much you need for quantity)
2 sticks of butter
1 cup of brown sugar
A bag of chocolate chips, 12 ounces
Toppings: almonds, walnuts, salt, cranberries etc
Method:
Set the oven at 180C
Line a baking tray with parchment paper
Arrange the matzah so that there's one layer of it
For carmel: add butter and sugar in pot over the stove, cook until it boils and let boil for 3
minutes (keep stirring continuously)
Pour the caramel over the matzah and spread with a spatula
Bake for 12 mins
Let it sit for 1 min and then pour the chocolate chips over the matzah
Put it in the oven for a minute to soften the chocolate chips
Once it comes out, spread the chocolate with a spatula and add the toppings
Leave it out for a half hour and then put in the fridge or freezer
Once it's ready, break it into chunks (it needs to freeze for at least an hour)
