# Aloo Gobi (Indian Cauliflower and Vegetables)

> The Indian classic that turns humble veggies into a golden, aromatic masterpiece. Turmeric-stained fingers are a badge of honor. Your kitchen will smell like a Mumbai street market.

## Ingredients

### Vegetables
- 1 cauliflower, broken into large florets
- 2 potatoes, cut into cubes
- 1 red pepper, cut into strips
- Diced pumpkin or sweet potato
- 1 large onion, cut into thin rings
- 1 hot pepper, cut into rings (optional)
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 2cm ginger, grated
- 1 ripe tomato, cut into segments
- 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (optional)
- 1/2 tsp coriander seeds (optional)
- 1 cup cilantro and basil, finely chopped (mandatory!)
- Juice of half a lemon
- Olive oil

### Spiced Water
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp hot paprika

## Instructions

1. In a flat pot with a little olive oil (NOT hot yet), add onion, hot pepper, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds and coriander seeds
2. When pot starts to sizzle, add vegetables: cauliflower, potatoes, pumpkin, tomato
3. Mix the spiced water ingredients together
4. Pour spiced water over vegetables
5. Add the cup of herbs, mix well
6. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and cover
7. Cook 50 minutes on low heat
8. Taste and adjust seasoning
9. Transfer to oven without lid at 200Â°C for 30 minutes until golden
10. Garnish with more fresh herbs and squeeze lemon juice on top
