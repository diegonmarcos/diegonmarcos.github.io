# Laudae's Challah

> The golden, braided beauty that makes Friday nights magical. Fluffy, slightly sweet, and absolutely gorgeous. Your kitchen will smell like a bakery and your soul will thank you.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons of yeast
- 3 cups of warm water
- 1 tablespoon of sugar + 1/2 cup of sugar
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil (canola or olive)
- 1 large whisked egg + 1 egg for the glaze
- Flour (adjust to texture - about 1 to 1.5 bags for 20 people)

## Instructions

1. Put warm water in a bowl, add 1 tablespoon of sugar and sprinkle the yeast evenly over the water
2. Let the yeast activate for about 10 minutes until fluffy and bubbly on top
3. Add the oil, remaining sugar, salt and the egg - whisk together
4. Slowly add flour while mixing until you get a "shaggy dough" consistency
5. Flour a surface and knead the dough until it's springy
6. Let it sit for 2 hours
7. Braid as you wish and coat with egg glaze (add any decorations you want)
8. Let the braided bread rise for about 30 minutes (don't let it grow too much)
9. Preheat oven to 220°C
10. Bake for 10 minutes at 220°C, then reduce to 180°C for another 20 minutes
