# Homemade Granola

> The breakfast topping that costs a fortune at fancy stores but takes 30 minutes to make at home. Crunchy, nutty, and customizable. Your yogurt will never be the same.

## Ingredients

- 500g thick oats
- 4 cups coarsely chopped mixed nuts (pecans, walnuts, hazelnuts, cashews)
- Pumpkin seeds
- Sunflower seeds
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1/2 cup honey (or silan for vegan)
- A little salt
- Optional: coconut chips, dried bananas

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C
2. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl
3. Spread the mixture on one or two baking trays
4. Bake for 30 minutes total, BUT:
   - Every 10 minutes, take it out and mix/stir
   - Return to oven after stirring
5. Keep an eye on it - make sure it doesn't burn!
6. If adding coconut chips or dried bananas, add them in the last 10 minutes
7. Let cool completely before storing
8. Store in an airtight container
9. Eat with yogurt, milk, or by the handful straight from the jar
