# Stuffed Summer Vegetables

> Gorgeous eggplants and peppers bursting with herby, lemony, lentil-rice filling. This is the dish that makes grandmothers proud and dinner guests speechless. Worth every minute.

## Ingredients

### Base
- Olive oil for frying
- 8-10 eggplants, peeled with stripes (or substitute some with peppers)
- 500g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 white onion, cut into half circles
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- A small bunch of thyme
- 1 spoonful brown sugar
- Salt and black pepper

### Filling
- 2 tbsp orange lentils
- 2 tbsp black lentils
- 2 tbsp green lentils
- 1/2 cup rice
- 3 white onions, diced
- 3 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 1 garlic clove, crushed
- A little grated nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 spoonful brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 bunch mint, cilantro, and parsley, finely chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- Juice of half a lemon

### Sauce
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1 heaping tsp coarse salt
- Juice of 2 lemons
- About 500ml water
