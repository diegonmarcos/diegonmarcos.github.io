# Maya's Vegan Challah

> Who says challah needs eggs? This plant-based beauty rises to the occasion with whole wheat goodness. Stuffed with garlic or za'atar, it's basically bread heaven.

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 3 cups white flour
- 3 cups whole wheat flour
- 2 cups warm water
- 1/2 cup warm water (for yeast)
- 2 tablespoons yeast
- 2 tablespoons sugar + 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup oil

### Garlic Filling (optional)
- 1 head of garlic, peeled and pressed
- Salt
- Olive oil

### Za'atar Filling (optional)
- Garlic
- Onions, diced
- Peppers
- Za'atar
- Salt
- Olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix 1/2 cup warm water with 2 tbsp sugar, add yeast and let activate
2. Mix the remaining warm water with oil and sugar
3. Pour the yeast mixture in
4. Add flour, one cup at a time while mixing
5. Let the dough rise for 1-3 hours (it should at least double in size)
6. Add fillings if using, then braid
7. Turn the oven to 180Â°C
8. Put challah in when oven is hot
9. Bake for 45-60 minutes until golden brown
