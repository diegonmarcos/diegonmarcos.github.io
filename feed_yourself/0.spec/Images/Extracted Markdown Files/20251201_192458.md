# George's Zucchini Bread

> The sneaky way to eat vegetables for dessert. Moist, cinnamony, and genuinely delicious. Don't squeeze the zucchini - that moisture is your friend. Makes two loaves because one is never enough.

**Yield:** 2 loaves

## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour (spooned and leveled)
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 1 1/2 cups cane sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil, plus more for greasing
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 3 cups grated zucchini (about 2 medium - DON'T press or squeeze!)
- Walnuts for folding in and topping

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F)
2. Grease 2 loaf pans
3. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
4. In another large bowl, whisk together sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla
5. Add grated zucchini to wet mixture and fold to combine
6. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients
7. Mix until JUST combined
8. Fold in walnuts
9. Divide batter between the two prepared pans
10. Sprinkle more walnuts on top
11. Bake for 40-55 minutes
12. Test with toothpick - should come out clean
13. Tops should spring back when touched
