Ingredients:
Ptitim
Oil
• Finely chopped onion
Sweet paprika
Cumin
• Soup powder
Curcum (tumeric)
A bit of sugar
Salt to taste
Small package of tomato paste
Method:
We'll start with pouring a bit of oil into our pot (remember- Ptitim love oil) and fry the onions until golden.
Then we'll add a teaspoon of paprika, cumin, curcum (tumeric), a bit of soup powder, a bit of sugar and salt.
After that we add the tomato paste, and the pack of ptitim. We'll add boiling water according to the quantity written on the bag and cook them according to the instructions (about 6 min).
