Noa's tofu shawarma
**note: this is normally spice mix for 1kg of chicken breast or thighs, use the same amounts of
spices and marinate your chicken if you have time. Can dice and TI9'u the chicken or just bbq in slices.
Ingredients:
•
A block of tofu (300g)
•
2 heaped tsps of garlic powder
2 heaped tsps of sweet paprika
1 tsp turmeric
3/4 tsp (be careful with this) cumin
1 tsp ground coriander seed, not dried coriander (all g if don't have)
Cayenne pepper to taste
Salt to taste
Half a lemon
Method:
-
Mix all the spices in a bowl
Add enough olive oil to wet all the spices Squeeze in half a lemon
Squeeze out excess tofu water into sink
Grate the tofu straight into your spice mix Mix well
Spread out thinly onto a lined baking tray
Bake at 180 or 190C fan forced for about 15 to 20 minutes, keeping an eye on it until it's really crispy
Eat on its own or with hummus and Israeli salad in a pita to really fool yourself into thinking it's a shawarma
Enjoy
