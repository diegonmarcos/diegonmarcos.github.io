# Maya's Caesar Salad

> A vegan twist on the classic that'll make Julius proud. Creamy, garlicky, and absolutely addictive. Your lettuce has never had it so good.

## Ingredients

### Salad
- Lettuce
- Any other vegetables you want to add

### Dressing (ratio-based)
- 1 part canola oil
- 1/2 part olive oil
- 1 1/2 parts soy milk
- 1 part lemon juice
- Capful of vinegar
- 4-6 heads of garlic (yes, really!)
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Blend together canola oil, soy milk, and vinegar until very thick like mayonnaise
2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and salt to the blend
3. Wash lettuce and vegetables and put in bowl
4. Dress immediately before serving

# Laudae's Tomato Salad

> Simple, fresh, and bursting with Mediterranean vibes. The longer it sits, the better it gets. Patience is a virtue, but sneaking bites is human.

## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Olive oil (generous amount)
- Garlic
- Red onion
- Basil
- Chili peppers

## Instructions

1. Chop up the tomatoes and garlic and place in a bowl
2. Add a generous amount of olive oil
3. Add the onion and the basil
4. Let it sit, and stir (bring the bottom of the salad to the top) every 10 minutes
