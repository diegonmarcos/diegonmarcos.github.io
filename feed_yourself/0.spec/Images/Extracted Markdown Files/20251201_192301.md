Method:

Mae a well in the center of the flour and add the yeast mixture and oil Mix together with wooden spoon
Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5 minutes, until smooth and not sticky
Add more flour (1 tbsp at a time) if the dough is still sticky, or add a tsp of water at a time if the dough is too dry
Place the dough in an oiled bowl and cover with a clean towel Leave to rise for 45 mins
Divide the rough into 4 once risen and roll them out on a floured surface Add sauce, cheese and toppings and bake for 12 minutes at 220°C
