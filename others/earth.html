KML to Google Earth Loader

Paste the public URL of your `.kml` or `.kmz` file to view it in Google Earth.

Please enter a valid URL.

How it works:

  1. Your KML/KMZ file must be publicly accessible on the internet (hosted on a server).
  2. Paste the full URL into the box above.
  3. Click the button to generate a special Google Earth link and open it in a new tab.
  4. Google Earth will then fetch and display your file.
