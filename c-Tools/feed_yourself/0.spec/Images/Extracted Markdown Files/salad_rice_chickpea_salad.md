# Rice & Chickpea Salad

> The ultimate Middle Eastern comfort bowl with crispy fried onions on top. Warm spices meet fluffy rice in a dish that proves chickpeas are seriously underrated. Your new potluck superstar.

## Ingredients

- 250g basmati rice
- 375ml boiling water
- 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp cumin (seeds are better, but powder works)
- 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
- 240g cooked chickpeas
- 180ml sunflower oil
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 tbsp plain flour
- 2 tbsp parsley and coriander (use as much as you want!)
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Pour 1 tbsp olive oil into a pan with a tight fitting lid on high heat. Add rice and 1/4 tsp salt, stir as rice warms up
2. Add boiling water and reduce heat to very low. Cover and cook for 15 mins
3. Remove from heat, cover with a clean towel then the lid. Leave aside for 10 mins
4. Heat remaining olive oil on high heat - add cumin and curry powder. Add chickpeas and 1/4 tsp salt quickly (don't let spices burn!)
5. Stir for 1-2 minutes to heat chickpeas, then transfer to a big mixing bowl
6. Pour sunflower oil into a pan on high heat. Test with a piece of onion (should sizzle)
7. Mix onion with flour to coat. Fry in batches for 2-3 mins until golden, transfer to paper towel and sprinkle with salt
8. Add rice, herbs and fried onions to chickpeas. Stir, season with salt and pepper
9. Serve warm or at room temperature
