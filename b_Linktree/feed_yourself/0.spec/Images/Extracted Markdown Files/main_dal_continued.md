## Final Steps

1. Add cilantro, basil, lentils and salt to the pot
2. Cover everything with water (about 2cm above the dish)
3. Bring to a boil, cover the pot
4. Cook on low heat for about 40 minutes until softened
5. Taste and adjust seasoning
6. Add lemon juice if needed
7. Finish with coconut cream for extra creaminess

# Ptitim (Israeli Couscous)

> The beloved Israeli pasta that's basically tiny toasted pearls of happiness. Golden, slightly sweet, and impossible to stop eating. Your new side dish obsession.

## Ingredients

- 1 package ptitim (Israeli couscous)
- Oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- A bit of soup powder
- A bit of sugar
- Salt to taste
- 1 small package tomato paste
- Boiling water (as per package instructions)

## Instructions

1. Pour a bit of oil into pot (remember - ptitim love oil!)
2. Fry onions until golden
3. Add paprika, cumin, turmeric, soup powder, sugar and salt
4. Add the tomato paste and stir
5. Add the ptitim and mix to coat
6. Add boiling water according to package quantity
7. Cook according to package instructions (about 6 minutes)
8. Fluff and serve!
