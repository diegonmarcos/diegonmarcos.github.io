# Dal

> The ultimate comfort food that hugs your soul from the inside. Spiced lentils simmered to creamy perfection with sweet potato. India meets your kitchen in the best way possible.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups orange or yellow lentils (can mix with green and black)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 medium sweet potato or piece of pumpkin, cubed
- 1 large potato, cubed
- 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 1 spoonful tomato paste
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 spoonful brown sugar
- 1 bunch cilantro and basil (don't skip these!)
- 1 teaspoon coarse salt
- 1 tablespoon grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika (hot or sweet)
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon cardamom
- Juice of half a lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional, for heat)
- 1/4 cup coconut cream (optional, to finish)
- 1/4 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Wash lentils and soak in water for about 1 hour
2. Fry onion, sweet potato and potato cubes in 1/4 cup olive oil for about 5 minutes until lightly browned
3. Add the spices: cumin, cinnamon, turmeric, paprika, cardamom, ginger, tomato paste, garlic, sugar and tomatoes
4. Mix well until everything is coated and smells amazing
5. Add cilantro, basil, drained lentils and salt
6. Cover with water (about 2cm above the dish)
7. Bring to a boil, cover and cook on low heat for 40 minutes until soft
8. Taste and adjust seasoning, add lemon juice if needed
9. Stir in coconut cream for extra richness
