Laudae's Cheese and Onion Pie

Method:
250g Butter
1/2 bag Flour (eyeball it)
1 egg
Milk
Cube the butter and put in a bowl with the flour
Smoosh it all together with your fingers and until it creates a 'squishy mess' or a
'textured entity'
Whisk up an egg and place in a measuring cup and then double the amount of liquid in
the cup with milk
Put that in the flour butter mix and mix with your hands again
Put that in the fridge for at least an hour (but the longer the better)
Ingredients for filling:
2 average sized potatoes
2 average sized onions
1kg of grated cheese (yellow used in shaar laadam, but would be better if it was cheddar)

2 tsp mustard
⚫ 25-50g of butter
Method (Do this in a heavy metal pan):
Cut the onion and potatoes into 1cm cubes (this is important)
