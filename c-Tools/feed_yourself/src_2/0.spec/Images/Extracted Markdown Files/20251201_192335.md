2 cups orange or yellow lentils can be mixed with green and black lentils
1 large onion, diced
1 medium sweet potato or a piece of pumpkin cut into cubes
1 large potato cut into cubes
2 ripe tomatoes, diced
A spoonful of tomato paste
3 crushed garlic cloves
A spoonful of brown sugar
A bunch of cilantro and basil (don't give up)
A teaspoon of coarse salt
A tablespoon of grated ginger
A teaspoon of turmeric
A teaspoon of cumin
A teaspoon of hot or sweet paprika
Half a teaspoon of cinnamon
Half a teaspoon of black pepper
A quarter of a teaspoon of cardamom
Juice of half a lemon
Half a teaspoon of chili powder (only those who want it spicy) You can add a quarter cup of coconut cream at the end.
Wash the lentils, soak in water for about an hour
Fry the onion, the sweet potato cubes, the potatoes in a quarter cup of olive oil for about 5 minutes until they are lightly browned and soften a little.
Add the hot spices: cumin, cinnamon, turmeric, paprika, cardamom, ginger, tomato paste, crushed garlic, sugar and tomato, mix well until everything is coated and a good smell begins to spread.
